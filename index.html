about Arkenstone

Arkenstone is the second intra college fine arts competition that will be organized and conducted by Varna,the Fine Arts Club of TCE.The term ‘Arkenstone’ means the king’s jewel ,a symbol of the pride.The participants will compete among themselves to achieve the king’s jewel in this competition. Arkenstone consists of 5 different events all are of submission type(i.e.online events). Read the rule book for details of the events.The submission date is on or before 30-04-2022(6pm)
